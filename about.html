THE FACE BEHIND L’AMOUR NAILS

I am Sapana, originally from Nepal- the land of Mount Everest.

Business has always been a passion for me, and owning L’amour Nails NZ has been a dream come true. From a young age, I enjoyed painting and designing my own nails, as well as doing it for friends and family as a hobby.

A turning point came after a Whipple surgery in 2017, during recovery, I realized that doing nails and art work was my true passion. This led to master my skills and boost my confidence in the nail industry by attending Monaco Nail Academy. Since 2018, I have been a fully qualified and NZ certified nail artist, turning my passion into a profession.

I operate a home-based boutique nail studio, where I provide personalized premium manicure services tailored to each client's individual needs. I am passionate about creativity, and this is reflected in my hand-painted nail art.

I have furthered my education and skills through various courses and training programs at the Monaco Nail Academy, including Soft Gel Extension, Mixed Media & Hand Painted Nail Art, E-file, and IBX Treatment. I have also trained in Mozhzheri Russian Manicure, covering Russian Classic Manicure, French Manicure, and Macro Manicure. Additionally, I have taken courses with Natalie Holt in Builder Gel and Nail Art. I continually up-skill to stay up-to-date with current trends and provide the finest nail care services.

I am a pet lover and have a super cute fur baby- Rocco, who is friendly and loves to meet new people.

I am excited to bring your beautiful nail visions to life.

- Sapana Simkhada -

My happy place where creativity begins

L'amour Nails NZ is a premium boutique nail studio located in Takanini that offers high- quality manicure services in a personalized and relaxing atmosphere.

The salon uses top-notch, professional-grade products, including hospital grade disinfectant; The Gel Bottle for gel manicures and soft gel extensions which are vegan, cruelty-free, and free of harmful chemicals.

The priority at L'amour Nails NZ is ensuring the safety and hygiene of natural nails while delivering the best possible service.

Embrace your nails as jewels, not just tools.
