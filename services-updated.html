Popular Services

gel manicure

The most popular service at L’amour Nails.

The gel polish manicure includes cuticle tidy, buffing and shaping of the natural nails with a choice of gel polish colour finishing off with luxury Dadi cuticle oil.

It also includes removal of gel polish applied at L'amour Nails on your next reapplication.

Additional fee applies for removing another salon's work.

Soft Gel Extension

Nails extensions using pre-formed gel tips that are flexible and durable. Fast to apply giving you 3 to 4 weeks of gorgeous wear. Soak off easily like gel polish and feel very light weight but strong. Ideal for your daily wear or any special occasions.

Medium Almond, medium Coffin and long coffin shapes are available. Can also be tailored to choice of nail shapes and sizes.

builder gel overlay

Builder gel overlay (Builder In a Bottle- BIAB)- a natural nail overlay that aids to create structure on your nails providing extra protection and strength.

It can add strong layer to maintain your current length or helps to grow your natural nails.

It gives long lasting manicure while maintaining natural feel and lightweight that can be backfilled every 3 weeks and is suitable for almost everyone.

It also includes application of gel polish colour with luxury Dadi cuticle oil finish.

PS: L’amour Nails only backfill our own work. Additional cost will apply for removal other salons’ work.

add ons

Services that can be added to any appointment as per your choice.

nail art

Any designs from glitters to hand painted art of your choice that can be customised as per your needs.

add strength

Builder in a bottle (BIAB) is an extra strong gel base coat added to your gel polish manicure.

IBX

Strengthening and repairing treatment to enhance the layers of your nails. This amazing product remains permanently in the nail to support, protect and repair damaged nails.

removal

A complete removal of gel polish or builder gel extension. It is a removal process for those who are wanting bare nails.

Safe removal of gel polish or builder gel extension includes shape, buff to high shine and cuticle tidy leaving them healthy and damage free, and finally topped off with luxury Dadi cuticle oil.
